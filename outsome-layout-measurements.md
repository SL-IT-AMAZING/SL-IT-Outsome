## CSS Spacing Patterns

### Consistent Spacing Rules:
1. **Section Vertical Padding**: 96px top and bottom (consistent across all sections)
2. **Section Horizontal Padding**: 0px (full-width sections)
3. **Container Horizontal Padding**: 24px left and right (48px total gutters)
4. **Content Max-Width**: 1392px (1440px - 48px gutters)
5. **Bottom Section Extra Padding**: 144px (Section 6 has additional bottom padding)

### Gap Between Sections:
- Sections are stacked directly with no margin between them
- Vertical spacing is controlled by section padding (96px)
- Total vertical spacing between sections: 96px (bottom of one) + 96px (top of next) = 192px
